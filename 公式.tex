以下大写字母代表列，例如I指代第I列。\\
E13指具体单元格。\\
\begin{gather}
    \label{1}
    I=G*H \\
    \label{2}
    G=C*F\\
    \label{3}
    F=\frac{E}{E13}\\
    \label{4}
    H=\frac{C13}{G13}
\end{gather}
将(\ref{2})、(\ref{3})代入(\ref{1})，可得：\\
\begin{gather}
    \label{5}
    I=G*H=C*F*C13*G13=C*\frac{E}{E13}*\frac{C13}{G13}=\frac{C*E*C13}{E13*G13}
\end{gather}
其中：
\begin{gather}
    \label{6}
    G13=sum(G4:G12)=G4+G5+G6+G7+G8+G9+G10+G11+G12
\end{gather}
根据(\ref{2})，并将(\ref{3})代入(\ref{2})则
\begin{gather}
    \label{7}
    G4=C4*F4=C4*\frac{E4}{E13}=\frac{C4*E4}{E13}\\
    G5=C5*F5=C5*\frac{E5}{E13}=\frac{C5*E5}{E13}\\
    G6=C6*F6=C6*\frac{E6}{E13}=\frac{C6*E6}{E13}\\
    G7=C7*F7=C7*\frac{E7}{E13}=\frac{C7*E7}{E13}\\
    G8=C8*F8=C8*\frac{E8}{E13}=\frac{C8*E8}{E13}\\
    G9=C9*F9=C9*\frac{E9}{E13}=\frac{C9*E9}{E13}\\
    G10=C10*F10=C10*\frac{E10}{E13}=\frac{C10*E10}{E13}\\
    G11=C11*F11=C11*\frac{E11}{E13}=\frac{C11*E11}{E13}\\
    \label{15}
    G12=C12*F12=C12*\frac{E12}{E13}=\frac{C12*E12}{E13}
\end{gather}
将(\ref{7})至(\ref{15})代入(\ref{6})，则
\begin{multline}
    G13=\frac{C4*E4}{E13}+\frac{C5*E5}{E13}+\frac{C6*E6}{E13}+\frac{C7*E7}{E13}+\frac{C8*E8}{E13}+\\
    \frac{C9*E9}{E13}+\frac{C10*E10}{E13}+\frac{C11*E11}{E13}+\frac{C12*E12}{E13}
\end{multline}
即
\begin{equation}
    \label{17}
    G13=(C4*E4+C5*E5+C6*E6+C7*E7+C8*E8+C9*E9+C10*E10+C11*E11+C12*E12)/E13
\end{equation}
将(\ref{17})、代入(\ref{5})，则
\begin{equation}
    \label{18}
    I=\frac{C*E*C13}{E13*\frac{C4*E4+C5*E5+C6*E6+C7*E7+C8*E8+C9*E9+C10*E10+C11*E11+C12*E12}{E13}}
\end{equation}
化简(\ref{18})得
\begin{multline}
    \label{19}
    I=\\
    \frac{C*E*C13}{C4*E4+C5*E5+C6*E6+C7*E7+C8*E8+C9*E9+C10*E10+C11*E11+C12*E12}
\end{multline}
由上可得，I的值和E13的数值无关。
